Apply to Job

Paste a LinkedIn job posting URL or fill in manually — then create an optimized CV and cover letter.

How to use: Open the LinkedIn job in your browser → copy the full job description text → paste it below → fill in Company and Title → click ✦ Optimize CV or ✉ Cover Letter. Optionally paste the LinkedIn URL to save it as a reference link in the sheet.

Uses apimaestro/linkedin-job-detail actor — make sure it's activated in your Apify account. Takes ~5s.
